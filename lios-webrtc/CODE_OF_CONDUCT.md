# Code of Conduct

The LiOS project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1, as its code of conduct. The full text of the standards and enforcement
guidelines is available at that link.

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience
for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of background or identity. We
pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open and healthy community.

## Our standards

Contributors are expected to be considerate and professional: be respectful of differing
viewpoints, give and accept constructive feedback gracefully, and focus on what is best
for the project and the community. Behavior that a reasonable person would consider
inappropriate in a professional setting is not acceptable here.

## Reporting and enforcement

Concerns may be reported confidentially to the project maintainers, either through
GitHub's private vulnerability reporting on the [LiOS repository](https://github.com/cmriat/LiOS)
or by contacting a maintainer directly. Reports will be reviewed and handled discreetly,
following the enforcement guidelines of the Contributor Covenant.
